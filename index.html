How to Play BOLC: The game counts up sequentially starting from 1. Your goal is to enter the next number in the sequence before the timer runs out.

The Catch: If the upcoming number fits any of the rules below, you must press the BOLC button instead of typing the number:
• It is a multiple of 3 (e.g., 6, 9, 12)
• It is a multiple of 7 (e.g., 14, 21, 28)
• It contains the digit 3 (e.g., 13, 23, 31)
• It contains the digit 7 (e.g., 17, 27, 70)

Tip: You can use your physical keyboard to play! Type the numbers normally, and hit the Spacebar to trigger BOLC.*
